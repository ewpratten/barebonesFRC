-- Controller Layout --
( This file is used by team 5024 to keep track of uttons on the controllers )

EXAMPLE FOR POWERUP:

DRIVER:

LEFT_JOY - drive
LEFT_TRIGGER - cube in
RIGHT_TRIGGER - cube out
RIGHT_BUMPER - slowmode (hold)
BTN_A - close gripper
BTN_B - open gripper
BTN_X - reverse controls
BTN_Y - robot 180

OPERATOR:

DPAD_UP - elevator posistion (switch)
DPAD_RIGHT - elevator posistion (vault)
DPAD_DOWN - elevator posistion (ground)
DPAD_LEFT - climb
